Method

Pre-Cooking

1

Wash your hand and dice the onions in a separate plate.

2

Wash the meat and cook with seasoning, salt and onion.

3

Cut, rinse and cook the fish till it is 3/4 done.

4

Wash, slice and steam the vegetable.

5

Watch and grind the pepper.

Cooking

1

Add about 0.3 liters of palm oil to the hot pot.

2

Add pepper to the hot oil. Add the seasoning and salt. leave for 10 minutes.

3

Add the vegetable to the soup, stir then leave for 5 minutes.

4

Add meat, fish and crayfish and stir carefully. Cover the pot with lid.

5

Allow to boil for 5 minutes. Remove from fire and serve.
